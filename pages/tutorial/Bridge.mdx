# Bridge From STACO to BASE

### Bridge using STACO Bridge [Coming Soon / TBA]

1. Navigate to https://bridge.stacochain.com and connect your wallet to Ethereum.
2. Select currency: STACO (To Chain will auto fill).
3. On your first bridge transaction, approve STACO spending first. In Metamask, make sure to select Use Default as custom spending cap.
4. Enter the amount of STACO to bridge (or press Max to select your entire balance).
5. Press Next and review your transaction before confirming with your wallet.
8. Wait for the transaction to complete, switch your wallet to Staco Mainnet, your native coins will arrive soon!
